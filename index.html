MissionRovee

Rovee's Adventure Awaits You!

Get ready to explore an uncharted planet and find minerals to complete Rovee's mission! Enter commands using the keyboard to command Rovee towards success. But be careful, because Rovee's battery doesn't last very long. Once the battery dies, Rovee's mission comes to an end.

Collect the Minerals!

Intel reports that there are golden-like minerals located on the planet, and Rovee is here to collect them for analysis. Use the commands to your advantage so that Rovee can collect the minerals and head back home safely!

Keep Her Battery Charged!

A charger was left for you to use so that Rovee can charge its battery. Use it as much as you can, because that battery won't last forever! Make sure you keep an eye on its battery at all times!

F.A.Q.

What is MissionRovee?

MissionRovee is an alt-ctrl space game where your mission is to collect minerals for analysis and bring Rovee home! Communicate with Rovee as much as possible, telling her where to go and what to do, to ensure the best chance of survival! Keep in mind that Rovee has a limited amount of battery life, so if she dies, the mission is a failure!

How can I play?

MissionRovee will be available to download from Steam on April 24th, 2024!

What is an 'Alt-CTRL' styled game?

The term refers to a video game that is played with non-traditional controls. Some traditional controls are well-known to gamers, such as keyboards, mice, gamepads, and touchscreens. But with this game, we use only the keyboard to operate a rover, as suuposed to using something like a joystick.

In this alt-ctrl space adventure, you explore the world which inhabits minerals to collect for processing. Rovee's job is to roam around for them and bring the minerals back home!
