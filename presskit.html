Description

MissionRovee is an alt-ctrl space game where the player controls a rover through a terminal to explore the world and complete its mission of collecting the world's minerals.


History

Created by the first Game Design and Development Capstone Team from Ball State, Mission Rovee got its start by following Richard LeMarchand's 'A Playful Production Process' and was chosen as the game to pursue for the capstone project out of three distinct proposals. The initial vertical slice, (essentially a completed level meant to represent the full game), was reviewed by a board of industry professionals and was determined as the most viable game to pursue in the short amount of time we have.


Features

  • Use commands to control Rovee and explore the world!
  • Collect minerals to complete her mission!
  • Keep Rovee's battery charged or her mission might come to an early end.
