TRAILER


DESCRIPTION

MissionRovee is an alt-ctrl space game that let's players explore the world and collect minerals using a built-in terminal.

FEATURES

  • Use commands to move Rovee around the world!
  • Collect minerals to complete Rovee's mission! 
  • Stay cautious about Rovee's battery life depleting and utilize the charger! 

ABOUT THE GAME

The game was developed as part of a capstone project for graduating seniors. It was decided when we were in a previous class for pre-production, where everyone created different games, and MissionRovee, was named rover game at the time, was on top based on its originality!

ABOUT SPHERE PROVINCE GAMES

As part of the learning experience of launching a video game, we created Sphere Province Games LLC., a student-ran company that focuses on quality and agile development. This was needed for us in order to publish to sites like Steam and collect revenue.
